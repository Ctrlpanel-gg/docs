Translating

You can speak your mother tongue?
You understand a certain language very well?
Then we need exactly you for our translator team!

After a long wait, the controlpanel has now been equipped with a translation system.

Now we need people who help us translate!

How to help#

The Translation list can be found here https://crowdin.com/project/controlpanelgg

On this Website you will see every language we need to translate and the current progress. Feel free to contribute or correct any language you know.

If you have a suggestion for a language or want to bring in your skills on a certain language, feel free to contact us on discord!
